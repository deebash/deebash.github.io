Privacy Policy for Remote+ for Samsung Smart TV

Effective Date: Feb 01, 2026

Thank you for using Remote+ for Samsung Smart TV! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

a. TV Connection

The app is designed only to function as a remote control for Samsung Smart TVs on your local network.

When connecting to your TV, the app requests permission on the TV screen before establishing the connection.

The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from your TV.

b. Usage Data

We may collect limited non-personal usage data such as crash logs and performance information to improve app reliability and performance.

c. Advertising

The free version of the app uses Google AdMob to display banner advertisements.

AdMob may collect device identifiers and other information as described in Google’s Privacy Policy.

d. Purchases

The app offers a one-time in-app purchase to remove advertisements.

All payments are processed securely by the Google Play Store. We do not collect or store any payment or billing information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To connect and control your Samsung Smart TV.
  • To improve app performance and stability.
  • To display ads in the free version of the app.
  • To verify and manage ad-removal purchases.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.

AdMob may collect and process data to provide advertising services. You can opt-out of personalized ads via your device settings.

4. Data Security

The app communicates with your TV only over your local network. No remote servers are used for TV control.

We take reasonable steps to protect app data and ensure secure communication.

5. Your Rights

  • You can uninstall the app at any time to remove all app data.
  • You can opt-out of personalized ads from your device settings.
  • You can disconnect the app from your TV at any time from the TV’s device manager.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected within the app and will take effect immediately.
