Privacy Policy for Colorrr

Effective Date: Jan 01, 2022

Thank you for using Colorrr! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

a. Images

When you select a black-and-white image, the app uploads it to our server for colorization. The processed colored image is sent back to the app and saved on your device.

We do not retain your images after processing.

b. Usage Data

We may collect non-personal usage data to improve the app, such as crash reports and performance metrics.

c. Advertising

Colorrr uses Google AdMob to show ads in the free version of the app. AdMob may collect device identifiers and other data as described in Google's Privacy Policy.

d. Purchases

If you purchase the Pro version, we process payments through the app store (Google Play). We do not store any payment information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the colorization service.
  • To improve app performance and user experience.
  • To serve ads (free version only).
  • To process purchases and manage Pro version access.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

AdMob may collect and process data for ad personalization. You can opt-out of personalized ads through your device settings.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Images are transmitted securely, and no data is stored on our servers after processing.

5. Your Rights

  • You can delete the app at any time, which removes all saved images and app data from your device.
  • You can opt-out of personalized ads through your device settings.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and take effect immediately.
